Special Furniture & Wood Elements

PALROM Products manufactures specialized semi-finished and finished wood parts for the furniture, packaging, and construction industries. From customized keeplats to distancers and wooden thresholds, we engineer bespoke wood solutions to your design.

FSC® Certified Available On Request

Keeplat Spruce

Specially grooved spruce mounting slats and components used in construction framing and high-end timber packaging grids. Highly stable and lightweight.

Keeplat Beech

Durable, solid steamed beechwood keeplats. Offers extreme shear resistance and longevity for heavy-duty industrial framing and custom furniture mounts.

Distancers Color Mix

Color-coded wooden separator and distance blocks designed for packaging lines, shipping crates, and organized stock management.

Wooden Threshold

Calibrated door thresholds machined from dense beechwood. Provides structural protection and aesthetic transitions between flooring layouts.

Industrial Distancer

Solid wood spacer bars and blocks designed for timber drying kilns, logistics stackers, and specialized packaging structures.

Wood with Iron Component

Composite structural blocks combining seasoned beechwood with integrated steel connectors. Tailored for heavy packaging and machinery mounting crates.
