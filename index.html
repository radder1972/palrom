Welcome to PALROM

Direct from the Source: Quality & Reliability

Based in the wood-rich region of Brad City, Hunedoara, Romania, Palrom Products SRL operates a modern sawmill, extensive kiln drying facilities, and a fully equipped planing workshop.

Our unique vertical integration allows us to oversee every step of production. We source sustainable beechwood locally, process it, dry it to optimal moisture contents, and plane it to meet the strict guidelines of our European and international client base.

100% Sustainable

Sourced and processed with FSC® certification in our immediate surroundings.

Tailor-Made Quality

Sawn, cut, planed, and finished exactly to custom customer dimensions.

25+ Years of Excellence

Product Catalogue

Precision Beechwood Products

Explore our core product lines, manufactured from premium FSC® certified Romanian beechwood.

Dowels

Beechwood Dowel Pins & Rods

Available in small sizes starting at 3 mm up to large dimensions of 60 mm. Options include smooth sticks and spiral-rilled finishes for optimal glue adhesion.

  • Diameter: 3mm to 60mm
  • Finishes: Smooth / Rilled (6mm to 20mm)
  • Wood Type: Steamed or unsteamed beech
Explore Beechwood Dowels
Planed

4-Sides Planed Lumber

Planed to extreme tolerances, available in both rectangular and square sections. Sawn, dried, cut to size, and calibrated for DIY distribution and carpentry shops.

  • Sections: Square & Rectangular
  • Edges: Sharp edge, Radius 3, or Radius 6
  • Moisture: Kiln dried to 8-12%
Explore Planed Lumber
Profiles

Architectural Profiles & Mouldings

An extensive range of decorative mouldings for interior cladding, carpentry finishing, and furniture trims. Custom profile cutting is available for large runs.

  • Shapes: Semiround, Quarter round, Plinth
  • Customization: Corner profiles, Thread, Calbat
  • Retail: Pre-labeled with EAN codes
