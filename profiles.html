Profiles & Mouldings

PALROM Products designs and manufactures an extensive catalogue of beechwood architectural profiles and mouldings. Precision-milled to achieve a perfect, smooth surface, our mouldings are ideal for cabinetry trim, flooring finishes, and wall decoration.

FSC® Certified Available On Request

Semiround Profile

Perfectly rounded convex mouldings used as decorative cover strips, cabinet edging, and custom furniture embellishments. Delivers a soft, clean border.

Profile Strip

Straight, thin flat wood strip profiles. Commonly utilized as cover mouldings, panel framing, or simple gap covers in interior finish carpentry.

Profile Finishing (Variant 1)

Specialty decorative finishing moulding with elegant curvature. Designed for transition joints between walls, ceilings, or furniture cabinets.

Profile Quarter Round (Variant 1)

Classic 90-degree curved quarter-round profile, perfect for baseboard floor gaps, inside corner joints, and decorative carpentry casing.

Profile Finishing (Variant 2)

Alternate decorative border profile showing double-stepped details. Adds depth and a premium look to wood frames and door casings.

Profile Plinth (Variant 1)

Standard plinth profile used as a robust wall-to-floor baseboard trim. Protects walls while creating an elegant wood border accent.

Profile Corner (Variant 1)

L-shaped outside corner moulding. Provides protective cover and high durability to vulnerable wall edges or cabinetry corners.

Profile Corner (Variant 2)

L-shaped corner cover showcasing subtle edge rounding. Provides a smooth, safe external edge corner for wood claddings.

Profile Triangular

Clean, triangular corner-block profiles. Typically used for internal support framing, concrete chamfering, or sharp inside corner decorations.

Profile Quarter Round (Variant 2)

Larger format quarter-round wood trim designed to cover flooring transitions and floor expansion gaps with a smooth radius look.

Profile Thread

Specialty grooved or threaded moulding strip profile. Tailored for decorative cabinetry rails and sliding track accents.

Profile Calbat

Unique concave-carved cove moulding profile. Replicates historic moldings to add classic styling to high-end furniture and ceiling transitions.
